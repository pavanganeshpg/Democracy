## 📂 Repository Contents

This repository is organized as a complete, transparent, and auditable scientific research pack:

### 1. ⚙️ Computational Simulation Engine
*   **[`civilization_simulation.py`](./civilization_simulation.py)**: A high-fidelity, self-contained Agent-Based Monte Carlo Simulation in Python.
    *   Simulates $10,000$ voter agents over a **100-year timeline** across 10 rural and urban states.
    *   Models voters' assets, caste affinities, education, and susceptibility to algorithmic propaganda.
    *   Implements seven voting universes: First-Past-the-Post (FPTP), Approval Voting, Single Transferable Vote (STV), STAR Voting, Quadratic Voting, Unrestricted Liquid Democracy, and Satyagraha-Sahasra.
    *   Subjected to progressive, multi-decade adversarial attack waves (Caste Mobilization, Corporate Bribery, AI Propaganda).

### 2. 📊 Simulation Combat Outcomes
*   **[`simulation_combat_report.md`](./simulation_combat_report.md)**: Detailed empirical report logging the outcomes of the 100-year parallel simulation runs.
    *   Contains the comparative final metrics leaderboard (Civilization Score, Corruption Rate, Polarization, Trust).
    *   Documents the mathematical and systemic collapse of legacy plurality, ordinal, and token-voting universes.

### 3. 🛡️ Adversarial Red-Team Audits
*   **[`civilization_democratic_audit.md`](./civilization_democratic_audit.md)**: The aggressive, peer-reviewed red-teaming report.
    *   Deconstructs and exposes dangerous flaws in technocratic candidate screening (risk of bureaucratic/elite capture).

### 4. 🏛️ Final Constitutional Architecture (The Blueprint)
*   **[`final_democratic_synthesis.md`](./final_democratic_synthesis.md)**: The definitive, streamlined Democracy 4.0 blueprint for India.
    *   **The National Voting System**: STAR Voting (Score Then Automatic Runoff) to mathematically eliminate caste-based plurality mobilization.
    *   **The Tricameral Legislature**: The technical **Sahasra Council** (advisory drafting), the dynamically elected **Lok Sabha (STAR Assembly)**, and the sortition-based **Jan Sabha (Senate)** of 10,000 randomly selected citizens holding absolute budget and ratification veto power.
    *   **Ballot-Level Visual Disclosure**: Eliminates technocratic eligibility gates. Anyone can run, but their audited competence index (SCI), forensic criminal history, and assets are displayed directly on the kiosk screen as color-coded charts next to their symbol at the moment of voting.
    *   **Hybrid Physical-Cryptographic Audit**: Keeps voting physically isolated, simple, and secure (tactile screens + physical paper ballots), while utilizing zk-SNARKs and homomorphic encryption purely on a decentralized precinct audit ledger.


The engine will run 10,000 parallel agents across 10 states under the three phased attack waves, outputting a complete, step-by-step audit log of final civilizational scores and system performance.
