## SECTION 1: THE CORE PHILOSOPHICAL AXIOMS

The Consortium’s analytical engine is permanently bounded by twelve inescapable axioms of civilizational systems design:

1.  **Conservation of Power**: Power cannot be eliminated—only mutated.
2.  **Anti-Corruption Paradox**: Every anti-corruption mechanism creates new, less visible corruption vectors.
3.  **Decentralization Masking**: Every decentralization system creates hidden, concentrated centralization points.
4.  **Epistemic Exclusion**: Every competence filter inevitably risks elite capture and the creation of an epistemic caste system.
5.  **Information Warfare Domain**: Every information channel eventually becomes an active, weaponized battlefield.
6.  **Attention Concentration**: Human attention naturally clusters into steep power-law monopolies.
7.  **Irrational Core**: Human beings are emotional, tribal, symbolic, and irrational agents.
8.  **The Cybernetic Limit**: Human civilizations cannot be fully optimized mathematically without destroying moral agency.
9.  **Algorithmic Sovereignty**: Algorithms that control visibility and recommendation parameters are the true unelected sovereigns.
10. **Authoritarian Stagnation**: Systemic stability guardrails will quietly and steadily evolve into authoritarian control systems.
11. **Asymmetry Leverage**: Information asymmetry is the ultimate leverage point of civilization-scale power.
12. **Complexity Disfranchisement**: Any governance architecture too complex for ordinary citizens inevitably loses popular legitimacy.
